# The Master Reading List

Every doc in this docket ends with its own curated "Read more" section — this page pulls all of those
links into one place (duplicates removed, grouped by the doc they support). If you and your wife want
a shared reading queue, this is it. The ones worth reading **first** as a couple: the car-seat safety
sources under the family doc, the gas-vs-diesel and depreciation sources under RV 101, and the
Mobile Internet Resource Center material under the WFH doc.
