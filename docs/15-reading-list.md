# The Master Reading List

Every external source cited across the docket, deduped by URL, in one place. This is a
reference list, not new research — if a doc's own claim and this list ever seem to
disagree, the doc's inline citation wins.

Archived docs (`docs/archive/`) are out of scope here and keep their own source lists
inline — see each archived doc for its citations.

---

## Start here

The highest-value links if you only click 15 things: parks + reservations, and the
cost/value reality check.

1. [Recreation.gov](https://www.recreation.gov/) — federal campground booking (national parks, national forests); most-cited reservation portal in the docket.
2. [Recreation.gov — Tips for Making Campsite Reservations at Popular Locations](https://www.recreation.gov/articles/list/tips-for-making-campsite-reservations-at-popular-locations/281) — how the 6-month booking rush actually works.
3. [Milepost Planner — 6-month booking window explainer](https://milepostplanner.com/blog/recreation-gov-6-month-booking-window/) — plain-English walkthrough of the same system.
4. [CNN — "American national parks feeling the strain this summer," July 2026](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/10/travel/travel-national-parks-strain-summer-crowds) — why summer/peak-season national park camping is brutally competitive right now.
5. [RVIA — Board of Directors Approves Requirement to Add Vehicle Length Labels](https://www.rvia.org/news-insights/rv-industry-association-board-directors-approves-requirement-add-vehicle-length-labels-all-manufactured-units) — background on why RV length labeling (and length limits) matters for park fit.
6. [RVshare — National Parks RV Length Limits](https://rvshare.com/blog/national-parks-rv-length-limits/) — the length-limit reality that shapes the 30–35 ft target.
7. [Camper Report — What is the Best RV Size for National Park Campsites?](https://camperreport.com/best-rv-length-for-national-parks/) — companion sizing piece.
8. [Roadtrippers — The Guide to State Park Camping Reservations in All 50 States](https://roadtrippers.com/magazine/state-park-camping-reservations/) — state parks don't share one system; this maps the fragmentation.
9. [AOL — "Most RV owners use their rig just 21 nights a year. Here's what that actually costs them"](https://www.aol.com/most-rv-owners-rig-just-140007328.html) — the core honest-math data point behind buy-vs-rent thinking.
10. [RVTravel — "RV buyer's remorse: Why 70 percent of RVers regret their purchase"](https://www.rvtravel.com/rv-buyers-remorse-1201/) — the sobering counterweight before committing.
11. [RVIA — 2025 Go RVing RV Owner Demographic Profile](https://www.rvia.org/2025-go-rving-rv-owner-demographic-profile) — industry-level usage/ownership baseline.
12. [MyDepreciation — RV Depreciation Calculator](https://www.mydepreciation.org/calculators/rv) — used-buy math: what a motorhome is actually worth over time.
13. [RV Loan Wizard — RV Depreciation by Type and Age](https://rvloanwizard.com/guides/rv-depreciation-by-type-and-age) — companion depreciation curve by class.
14. [BestRV — RV Rental Costs 2026: Complete Breakdown + Hidden Fees](https://www.bestrvrentalcompanies.com/guides/rv-rental-costs) — what renting first (before buying) actually runs.
15. [NRVIA — National RV Inspectors Association](https://nrvia.org/) — the credentialing body for pre-purchase inspectors; start here before any used-RV offer.
